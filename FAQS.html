Frequently Asked Questions

What features does the weather app offer?

Breezify provides current weather conditions, hourly and daily forecasts, sunrise and sunset times, air quality index (AQI), and notifications for severe weather alerts.

How often is the weather information updated?

Weather data is updated in real-time from trusted sources and refreshes every hour, ensuring accuracy for current conditions and forecasts.

Can I use the weather app without an internet connection?

An internet connection is required to fetch the latest weather data. However, previously loaded information might be available offline until refreshed.

Is the weather app free to use?

Yes, our weather app is free to use. Some advanced features or extended forecasts might require an upgrade to a premium version.

Does the app provide weather data for any location worldwide?

Yes, the app supports worldwide weather tracking. You can search for any city or use GPS to get your local weather information.

How does the app determine my location?

The app uses GPS and network-based location services on your device to provide local weather. Make sure to enable location permissions for accurate data.

Why isn’t my location updating automatically?

Location updates require GPS and internet access. Check your permissions and connectivity, or manually refresh the app to update your location.

How accurate are the forecasts?

Our forecasts come from reliable sources and are generally accurate. However, sudden weather changes may not always be reflected immediately.

Can I customize units of measurement?

Yes, you can toggle between Celsius and Fahrenheit for temperature, and choose kilometers or miles for wind speed under the settings.

Why is the sunrise/sunset time incorrect?

Ensure that your device time zone is set correctly. The app automatically adjusts sunrise and sunset times based on your location and time zone.

Do I need an account to use the app?

No account is needed for basic weather features, but signing up may unlock personalized settings, favorite locations, and weather notifications.

What data does the app collect from me?

The app collects location data for weather updates but does not store any personal information unless you create an account.

Can I manage or delete my personal data?

Yes, you can manage or delete your account and data under the privacy settings. We take user privacy seriously and comply with data protection regulations.

Why is the app not loading weather data?

Check your internet connection, and ensure the app has permissions for location and network access. Restarting the app can also resolve some issues.

The app keeps crashing; what should I do?

Ensure you’re using the latest version of the app. Clear the app’s cache or try reinstalling it. If the problem persists, contact support.

I received a weather alert but don’t see any warnings.

Weather alerts are sometimes issued preemptively. It may take a few minutes for the app to display all related information.
